MY FAVOURITE RECIPES

  • Comforting Curry Noodle Bowls
  • Mexican Caesar Salad
  • Mini Mason Jar Fruit Crisps

Comforting Curry Noodle Bowls

  • This simple curry soup has been one of our quick and easy favorites.
  • It is full of bold flavors, easy to customize with your fave protein (shrimp, tofu, chicken, etc.), and sooo cozy and delicious.

Mexican Caesar Salad

  • Two of my all-time faves , Caesar salads and Mexican food , came together in this fabulously delicious salad .
  • Inspired by the Mexican Caesar at one of our favorite restaurants in Kansas City.
  • I especially love the pepitas and crunchy tortilla strips sprinkled on top.

Mini Mason Jar Fruit Crisps

  • I have made dozens and dozens of fruit crisps in my life.
  • And we could not get enough of them!
  • The recipe is easy to customize with any juicy fruits that you have on hand, and easy to scale for 2 people or up to a large crowd.
